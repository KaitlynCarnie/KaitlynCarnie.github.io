VOL 2. THE INTIMACY OF TWO
Modern dating has a new look in the 21st century. We reach out for intimate connections through technology. To meet our soulmate, we don't even have to leave the house. While modern technology has the ability to connect two people from different sides of the world, it also causes the loss of connection to people within reach.
